## 🎁 Discount on 2nd Batch - Impact Analysis

### 📊 Scenario:

- **Current Stock:** 100 units @ ₹100 each
- **New Purchase:** 50 units @ ₹120 each
- **Supplier gives 20% discount on 2nd batch**

### 💰 What Happens:

#### **Without Discount:**

- Purchase Price: ₹120 per unit
- Average Cost: ₹106.67
- Sale Price (50% margin): ₹160.00

#### **With 20% Discount:**

- Purchase Price: ₹120 - 20% = **₹96 per unit**
- Average Cost: ₹98.67
- Sale Price (50% margin): **₹148.00**

### 🎯 **Amazing Benefits:**

#### **1. Direct Savings:**

- Save ₹24 per unit × 50 units = **₹1,200 total savings**

#### **2. Lower Customer Prices:**

- Sale price drops from ₹160 to **₹148**
- **₹12 savings per unit for customers**

#### **3. Competitive Advantage:**

- Can sell 7.5% cheaper than without discount
- Maintain exact same 50% profit margin
- Better market position

### 📈 **Discount Scale Impact:**

| Discount | New Price | Average Cost | Sale Price | Customer Saves |
| -------- | --------- | ------------ | ---------- | -------------- |
| 5%       | ₹114      | ₹105.33      | ₹158.00    | ₹2.00          |
| 10%      | ₹108      | ₹104.00      | ₹156.00    | ₹4.00          |
| 15%      | ₹102      | ₹102.67      | ₹154.00    | ₹6.00          |
| 20%      | ₹96       | ₹98.67       | ₹148.00    | ₹12.00         |
| 25%      | ₹90       | ₹96.00       | ₹144.00    | ₹16.00         |

### 🏆 **Key Insight:**

**Supplier discounts are AMAZING with average method!** Every rupee you save in purchase cost directly translates to:

- Lower average cost
- Lower sale prices
- More competitive positioning
- Same profit margin percentage
- Happy customers

### 💡 **Strategy:**

Always negotiate for bulk discounts and pass the benefits through average costing - it's a win-win for you and your customers!
